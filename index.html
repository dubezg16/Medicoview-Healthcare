Our Story

Medicoview Healthcare Services (Dental) is a modern dental centre located in Ejigbo, Lagos. We were founded on the belief that premium dental care should be accessible, comfortable, and available whenever you need it.

Unlike traditional clinics with restricted hours, we understand that dental pain doesn't adhere to a schedule. That is why we stay open 24 hours a day, ensuring you always have a place to turn to for relief and professional care.
